A ROGO AI clone for the Saudi market. The name is Faheem. Targeting investors and banks. Goal is minimize time between research and investment and to get to results very quickly.

The goal is NOT to build a real product, the goal is to do a DEMO for the hackathon judges showing a singls user story with sleek nice interface. The data will be static for now (you will gather the data for a single Saudi company, preferably public).


# User Story

A company named Lunar Investments wants to evaluate a certain business: 
- They contact us (Faheem) first to understand their needs and agree on the subscription model
- They then go through on boarding process interfaces (web-based), connecting to certain api's and data sources (see the screenshots)
- They then ask the Faheem AI questions and get answers WITH REFERENCES to the docs. The references are clickable
  - If the prompt is short/bad, a button appears to "improve" the prompt (use haiku or weak model for this)
- They also upload/add their branded templates for presentations/excel/words
- They get outputs in their own branded documents using their own data, like their own risk profile, protofolio, investment appetitie
- Team research: search online for team stuff
- for the demo build using claude ai
- once all done, they get word/presentation or whatever they asked for with the company details. They can even provide their own examples
- EVERY NUMBER AND EVERYTHING HAS A SOURCE (RAG/KNOWLEDGE BASE/WEB). VERY IMPORTANT TO WIN THE HACKATHON
- For this case, use a public company in Saudi, download its data to a folder (pdfs etc), and reference them in your answer. Also generate powerpoint/word templates using our branding as our outputs (like an investment firm looking to eval a company/invsetment)
- You will also generate full excel financial analysis to in the example.
- you must mention that we can connect data rooms, news sources, and we have multiple agents (legal, risk, compliance, hr, investment analysis)
